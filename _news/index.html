What a great time of year! Celebrating the Birth of Christ and the New Year are just around the corner, and here at Portland Christian Center we want to thank you for your prayers and your generous donations. Before this year is officially wrapped up, we want to show you just a few of the things we’ve accomplished together in 2009:

  • WE SERVED... in homeless shelters, senior centers, assisted living homes, the Union Gospel Mission, Portland Rescue Mission, Royal Family Kids Camp, Young Champions, Salvation Army, Robert Gray Middle School, and more!
  • WE SUPPLIED... 200 backpacks stocked with school supplies to under-privileged children in Portland.
  • WE TRAINED... over 150 new parents in our community through Love and Logic parenting classes.
  • WE PROVIDED... 3,000 meals to the homeless in downtown Portland.
  • WE TOUCHED... 15,000 people through our Christmas Celebration Concerts with the support of 500 faithful volunteers.
  • WE CELEBRATED... with participants of our Financial Peace Seminar who paid off $233,330.00 of personal debt after attending one of our financial classes.
  • WE LAUNCHED... Rapid Deployment Teams to repair cars, move/evacuate single moms, provide free financial counseling to widows, and broker asset sales to stave off financial hardship.
  • WE SPONSORED... 10 water wells that enabled thousands of Ethiopians to enjoy safe drinking water and sent a team to help build a school in Panama.
  • WE SUPPORTED AND TRAINED... leadership for 1,397 church plants and 70,000 new Ethiopian Christians.
  • WE GAVE... $400,000 to spread the good news of Christ around the world through our global missions program.

Your support and generosity are what make it possible for us to continue meeting needs, showing love and sharing God’s hope with the world. And the future is filled with hope for so many more.

We are excited to see all God will do through us in the final months of 2009 and and even more with your help as we move into 2010!
