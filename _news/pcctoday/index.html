College Campus Visiting Opportunities

Interested in Becoming a Marriage Mentor?

Marriage Mentoring is linking seasoned couples with less experienced couples for an amazing experience that pays huge dividends for both couples. Does that sound like something you would be interested in as a mentor couple or mentoree couple?

We are currently looking for married couples that would be interested in being trained as mentors and linked to other couples in the church. This would include a weekend training and mentor certification with Pastor Ray and Linda Noah before being connected to another couple. You will complete diagnostics, be given powerful tools to lead your mentoree couple, and experience the joy of your own relationship being enriched. For more information take a look at our new website under Life Learning. There you will find an application to express your interest in this exciting new ministry! You may also contact Pastor Linda Noah [ email ] or David and Deborah Kaiser.
