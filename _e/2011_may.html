Different Children, Different Needs

Material Fee: $20.00 for individual; $35.00 for couple
7:00-8:30 PM in Rooms 229-230

The key to effective parenting is discovering what motivates your child to respond the way he or she does. Different Children Different Needs is one of those “Ah Ha” classes that gives you practical answers to dealing with all of your relationships, but particularly as it relates to your children. Through the use of the scientific diagnostic, the DiSC profile, we will go on one of the most enlightening journeys of your life. Tied into the teaching are plenty of practical Love and Logic tools to handle any unique temperament in powerful and positive ways! Led by Linda Noah and Amy Slater.

Kids’ Church Theme for May: Forgiveness

Sundays in May from 10:15-11:45 AM in the Trailhead

Kids, join us in May as we learn all about forgiveness. That means, deciding that someone who has wronged you doesn't have to pay. Our memory verse this month is Colossians 3:13 - "Forgive the things you are holding against one another. Forgive, just as the Lord forgave you."

Here's what we'll be talking about each week:

  • May 1: Since God forgives you, you should forgive others. (Jesus’ parable of the unmerciful servant, Matthew 18)
  • May 8: When people are forgiven, it can change them. (Jesus and Zacchaeus, Luke 19)
  • May 15: When you want to get even, do the opposite. (Joseph and his brothers, Genesis 45)
  • May 22: Don’t wait to start making things right with others. (Jesus teaches about forgiveness, Matthew 5)
  • May 29: BGMC Emphasis Sunday
