Love and Logic: Early Childhood Parenting Made Fun!

Beginning January 12th / 7:00 pm in Rooms 229-320

It’s never too early to use Love and Logic; it even works with the teeny tiny ones! Turn the “terrible twos” into the “terrific twos” with easy to use steps. Learn great solutions to:

   * “I waaaaant it!” “You can’t make me,” and a favorite, the grocery store “gimmes.”
   * Show your tots that whining and arguing does not pay
   * Put an end to misbehavior in public
   * Smooth-out mornings and bedtimes
   ...and LOTS more!

Class includes a workbook, handouts, small group interaction, large group lecture and DVD presentation from Dr. Charles Fay, Jim Fay and Dr. Foster Cline. Facilitated by certified instructor, Linda Noah and Angie Mitchell.
