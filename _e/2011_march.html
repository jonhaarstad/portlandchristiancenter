Reach

4000 Lives Changed

Portland Christian Center has partnered with Latin America Child Care for decades, building schools, churches, and sponsoring children whose parents cannot afford to send them to school. Recently, one of our missionary families working in El Salvador, Bob & Cilia Bueno, shared a report on the effect L.A.C.C. is having in their host country in only one year. Through the course of 2010, nearly 16,000 students attended an L.A.C.C. school in El Salvador. Of those students, 4000 accepted Christ. Portland Christian Center, in partnership with our missionaries and other Assembly of God churches in the U.S. and El Salvador is impacting a country, a community, families and individuals for eternity. Our investment dollars are reaping a harvest every minute of the day. If you would like to know more about Latin America Child Care, log on to www.latinamericachildcare.org or contact Bob Ward [ email ].

Kids Church Theme for March: Courage

Sundays in March from 10:15-11:45 AM in the Trailhead

Kids, join us in March as we learn all about courage: that means, being brave enough to do what you should, even when you’re afraid.

  • When you’re afraid, remember God is with you. Because God was with David, he had the courage needed to face a giant problem (1 Samuel 17).
  • Remember God is with you when you do the right thing. God gave Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego the nerve to defy the king despite fiery consequences (Daniel 3).
  • Remember God is with you when you speak up for what’s right. God gave Esther the boldness to stand up and speak up for some people in dire straits (Esther 4-8).
  • Remember God is with you when you are the only one doing the right thing. Daniel faced the lions with courage because God gave him the strength to do so (Daniel 6).
