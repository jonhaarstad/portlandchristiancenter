Dear Church Family,

After a national search for a Children's Pastor, we are pleased to announce Philip and Chanda Hahn have been invited to join our Pastoral team as our new pastors in this vital area.

The Hahns hail from St. Joseph, Michigan, where they served on the pastoral staff in an Assembly of God church. Philip and Chanda have been Children's Pastor for the last 8 years and come with a wealth of passion for and experience with children. Their skills include administrating volunteers, training workers, teaching, preaching, worship leading, website development, creating and editing ministry video, puppetry, illusions, set construction, piano, guitar, singing, songwriting, writing curriculum, and most of all loving children!

They have hands-on experience in almost every area of children's ministry including Sunday School, Kid's Church, JBQ, Kids Choir, Awana, Community Children's Outreaches, Christmas productions, Christian Kids Television, Christian Karate, Royal Family Kids Camp, Crusades, Kid's Evangelism teams...and more!

The Hahns have been married for nine years and have twins, Aiden and Ashley, born June 3, 2008. Philip was raised in the ministry as the son of a Children's Pastor, so he has been saturated in children's ministry his entire life.

Phillip and Chanda are excited to become a part of our church family. We will be creating opportunities for you to get to know them better in the coming weeks. Meanwhile, please join me in giving them a warm Portland Christian Center welcome! They are currently traveling from Michigan and will be officially on staff by the middle of next week. We will introduce them to you on Sunday, December 11.

Soli Deo Gloria,

Pastor Ray
