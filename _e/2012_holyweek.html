Entering
Holy Week

at Portland Christian Center

This Sunday begins Holy Week at Portland Christian Center. Holy Week is the week immediately preceding Easter or Resurrection Sunday. It is observed in many Christian churches as a time to commemorate and enact the suffering (Passion) and death of Jesus through various observances and services of worship.

We invite you to join with us this week as we focus our attention on what Jesus did for us. We look forward to seeing you!

April 1st
Palm Sunday

Join us at 9:00AM or 10:45AM as we gather for this special Easter Week Sunday with a message from Pastor Ray and a time of water baptism as we celebrate with those that have chosen to make a public declaration of their faith.

April 5th-Easter Sunrise
Prayer & Fasting

After washing the feet of the disciples, Jesus celebrated the Last Supper, instituting Holy Communion and declaring the mandate to “love one another as I have loved you”. This all occurred on what has come to be known of as "Maundy Thursday", also known as "Holy Thursday". Join us for this 3-Day Prayer & Fasting experience from Thursday (April 5th) through Easter Sunday morning (April 8th). Click here for tips on fasting.

April 6th
7:14 Good Friday Communion & Prayer

Christians around the world commemorate the passion, suffering, and death on the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ on Good Friday. Come on Good Friday at 7:14PM (Sanctuary) for a time of reflection and communion. Our Good Friday service will rest on God’s promise in 2 Chronicles 7:14 "...if my people...will humble themselves and pray...I will heal their land...”
