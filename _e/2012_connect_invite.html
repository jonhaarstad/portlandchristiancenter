CONNECT 101:
Connect With My Church Family

CONNECT 101 is designed to clearly explain God's hand on Portland Christian Center and plans for the future. You'll learn about our beliefs, our statements of purpose, and our church's strategy and structure. You'll also hear about the history of our church, how the five biblical purposes are necessary to fulfilling God's calling for your life and how you can play a part in what God is up to at PCC. At the end of CONNECT 101, you are invited, but not required to become members. Membership allows you to participate in the Annual Business meeting on March 11. More info

CONNECT 201:
Connect With Habits of Simple Faith

CONNECT 201 focuses on establishing habits that promote spiritual growth and maturity. We will take a look at the growth process of your life with Christ and how to take the next step to become more like Christ. Special attention is given to understanding the what, why, when, and how of four core habits: Prayer, Daily Time With God, Tithing, and Fellowship. This inspiring class is excellent for those who are looking for practical ways to grow or refresh their walk with Christ. More info

CONNECT 301:
Connect With My S.H.A.P.E.

In CONNECT 301, through diagnostic tools, activities, and discussion, you will develop a personal understanding of God's workmanship in your life by learning your S.H.A.P.E. (Spiritual Gifts, Heart, Abilities, Personality, Experiences). Special attention is directed towards finding ways to use your gifts (S.H.A.P.E.) to impact the Kingdom of God in profound ways as you express your gifts and talents for the glory of God. More info
