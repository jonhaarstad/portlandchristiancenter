Student Missions Auction

Join us for our annual Student Missions Dinner & Auction on Sunday, April 29 from 5:00 - 8:30 PM! Every dollar raised will go to support our students for their summer missions trips. Auction items include vacation packages, gift baskets, event tickets, restaurant certificates, sporting goods, furniture, household, lawn and garden items, home electronics, toys, service certificates, and much more! Cost is only $8/adult (includes dinner, bidder number, and auction catalog) and $5/child. *Childcare is provided!
