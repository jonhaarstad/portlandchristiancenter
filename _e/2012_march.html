Holy Week - Prayer and Fasting

We experienced God's presence, heard God's voice, and received God's power in fresh and life-changing ways during our January "Week of Prayer & Fasting". Now again, we want to make ourselves available to God as a spiritual family during Holy Week to seek His face, experience the love that led His Son to die for our sins, and receive a fresh revelation of Christ's resurrection power. Join us in this experience that will begin sundown of "Maundy Thursday" (April 5) through sunrise of Easter Sunday (April 8). Encounter the Risen Savior like never before.
