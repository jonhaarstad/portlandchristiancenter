New Members Class

October 10, 5:00 – 7:30 PM

Since we have members from many different church backgrounds, our Membership Class is designed to clearly explain our church structure and beliefs, as well as our vision for the future of this ministry. This is a great time to get to know Pastor Ray Noah, as well as various deacons and pastoral staff. Upon completion of our Membership Class, you are invited, but not required, to become a member of Portland Christian Center and join a family growing to become more like Christ.

Weekly Intercessors’ Meeting with Pastor Ray

Tuesdays, 12:00 PM, beginning October 5

Join Pastor Ray for an hour each Tuesday in the Hospitality Center for intercessory prayer. We will be praying for revival in our city, renewal in our church, effectiveness in our mission outreaches, and for the needs of our congregation. If you have a passion to see spiritual breakthroughs in these areas, the Intercessors’ Meeting is for you!

Holiday Frugalista

November 5, 7:00 – 9:00 PM

Ladies get ready to Dine, Dress and Design on a Dime. Come and learn how to become a Holiday Frugalista at our fall event this November! We'll feature a fashion show with clever ideas to update your holiday wardrobe for $10 and under, a chance to participate in a purse swapping event, and make-up consultants will help complete your look. Sample fabulous food and learn how to present your own "tiny bites". Find frugal ways to revamp furniture and household accessories for the holidays. We’ll show you how to stretch your dollars in ways that will astound you! Bring a friend!

Upward Basketball

Registration Now Open!

Kids Praise Company!

Rehearsals: Wednesday evenings from 6:00 – 7:00 PM

All kids 1st-5th grade are invited to join us for the most awesome kids choir on the planet! KPC kids have a blast while being taught musical skills and concepts, earning fun prizes, and learning to sing a great collection of Christmas songs that speak of the true meaning of Christmas. Kids have the opportunity to perform in Portland Christian Center's annual Christmas Celebration Concerts where fifteen thousand people come each year to hear fantastic music! In addition to the concerts, KPC will also have the amazing opportunity to be included in a recording project for Christmas! This is definitely a choir you don't want your child to miss out on! Registration is only open until October 13th, so hurry and sign your child up today!
