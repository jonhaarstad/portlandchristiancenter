Women's "Christmas Tour of Homes"

Tuesday, December 13th - 6:00 - 9:30 p.m.
Ladies, we're doing a NEW thing! Grab a friend and come to our Christmas Tour of Homes. We will begin the evening at church with a light appetizer, holiday music, instructions for the evening and...a SWAP!! Bring a holiday decoration that is still in good condition that you are no longer using in your home and swap it for something from someone else's home. Then, maps will be handed out and our fun will continue as we sample different goodies at each home that we visit. As we explore, you'll be able to observe all kinds of creative decorations and get some new craft and gift ideas just in time for those last minute holiday events. Oh, and we'll have lots of great door-prizes, too!

Cost: $10 (after December 4th, price will increase to $15). This event is limited to 175 participants. Sign-up in the church lobby or online. More Info & Register

Announcing A2: Young Adults in Biblical Community

STARTING IN JANUARY 2012!
Coming January 2012, our Young Adult ministry is launching a fresh way to grow in Biblical community as modeled for us in Acts 2:42-47 and with that is an official name change to "A2". The focus will be on forming community opportunities in local homes in the Portland area to promote the Biblical values of growing, reaching, worshipping, serving, and belonging together. We're moving from Sundays to Friday nights at 7:00 p.m.. We'll meet in homes every Friday night with a big “First Friday” service every month together at the church. A2 promises to be an incredible, new opportunity for young adults to gather and grow in Biblical community!
