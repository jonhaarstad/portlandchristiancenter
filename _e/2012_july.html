LifeSkills: Real Character

Real Characters? That's for sure! Joseph, Abraham, and Jacob are not just Bible Characters, they're real people with real issues like ANGER, PRIDE, and RESENTMENT. We will take a look at their lives and how they dealt with, overcame, and sometimes failed in the sins we all seem to wrestle with. Explore what living under the transforming power of Jesus can be. Join us Sundays at 9:00 AM in the Choir Room. [ More Info ]
