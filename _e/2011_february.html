Children

Kids Church Theme for February: Kindness

Sundays in February from 10:15-11:45 AM in the Trailhead

Kids, join us in February as we learn all about kindness...that means, showing others they are valuable by how you treat them! We'll learn it's important to:

  • Do to others what you want them to do to you. The gold standard of kindness (Luke 6)
  • Treat others the way Jesus would treat them. Jesus gave others (including kids!) His time and attention (Mark 10)
  • Treat others as if they belong to God. When you do, it's as if you're treating Jesus that way (Matthew 25)
  • Do for your enemies what they wouldn't do for you. Give them another chance (Acts 9)

Royal Family Kids’ Camp

Portland Christian Center will sponsor its 11th Royal Family Kids’ Camp this July. Over the past 10 years we have been offering this camp, Portland Christian Center has been able to make a tremendous difference in the lives of over 500 neglected, abused and forgotten foster children in the Portland Metro Area. On Sunday, February 27th, we will be taking a special offering during the Celebration Service to help raise funds for this year’s camp.
