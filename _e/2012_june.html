Special Father's Day Sunday - June 17

Join us as we celebrate our fathers together on Sunday, June 17. During this special Sunday morning, we'll welcome back special guest Jaime Jamgochian who will lead us in a time of worship. Then, we'll enjoy a message brought to us by Rev. Jim Swanson, former pastor of PCC.

A Summer Wednesday Class for Everyone!
"Transformational Leadership" - home / church / work

Join us Wednesday nights from June 27 - August 8 at 7 PM for "Transformational Leadership", a class designed to develop your leadership skills at home, at church, and at work. We're also offering this as a three day workshop June 21-23. [ More Info ]

Young Champions Sports Camp - Track & Field for Kids

Hurry and grab your spot for one of this summer's big events. This camp, for kids ages 3½ - 5th grade, features one-on-one coaching, a free t-shirt, track & field clinics, stadium seating for families and one big track meet day with fun for the whole family including a BBQ, snow cones and a bounce house. Early-bird registration is ONLY $15/child for this 4-Day event. This is a great opportunity to invite your friends and neighbors!
[ More Info & To Register ]

Student Choir Return Concerts

Enjoy our student choirs' summer tour return concerts. The Witness Middle School Choir will sing Wednesday, June 20th, at 7:00 PM in the Sanctuary and then, on Sunday, June 24th, join us for the Priority High School & College Choir Concert at 9 & 10:45 AM during our Sunday services.

Join a Backyard BBQ This Summer!

Join us this summer for our monthly BBQ dinners hosted by families in our church. These events are designed to help you meet new people and enjoy time together. Sign up at the table in the lobby on Sunday mornings. The first BBQ week is June 17-23. Contact: Tana Hines [ Sign Up to Host ]

Holy Spirit Day

Join us Saturday, June 9th from 9 AM - 3 PM in the Hospitality Center. Cost is $10 per person which includes a continental breakfast & lunch. Register here or at the Congregational Life table in the church foyer on Sunday. Everyone is welcome. Contact: Tana Hines [ Register ]

"Master's Men" Tuesday Small Group

Beginning Tuesday, June 5 at 6:00 AM, join us for our new 8-week study based on "Courageous", a movie about four police officers who learn what it takes to be a man of honor on the job and at home. Start your day off right! Contact: Stephanie Van Raden [ More Info ]

Women's Summer Bible Study

Beginning Tuesday, June 5 from 9:30-11:30 AM in the Hospitality Center, join us for our 7-week study on the book of Nehemiah by Kelly Minter. This is a DVD study and workbooks can be purchased for $12.95. No childcare available. Contact: Tana Hines [ More Info ]
