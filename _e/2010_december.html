TITLE: "Designer Genes"
SCRIPTURE: Matthew 1:1-17

Matthew 1 lists the genealogy of Jesus-something most of us skip over when we read the Christmas story. But when you look more closely at it, you'll find God is there in all those “begats” ... and you'll discover some pretty cool stuff that applies to your life too. Join us this Sunday...

Ladies Bible Study:
Winter Term begins January 11

Tuesdays: 9:30 AM in the Chapel - Wednesdays: 7:00 PM in the Hospitality Center

Ladies, our winter term Bible study focuses on various characters of the Bible. Our Tuesday morning study is Learning Life Principles from New Testament Men of Faith. The Wednesday night study is Life Principles of the Women of the Bible. Please join us as we explore these noteworthy characters who learned the importance of building a relationship with God and lived completely surrendered to God's leading. Workbooks are $12.00 each and will be available for sale during the month of December both online and in the Lobby on Sunday mornings.

Side by Side Women’s Mentoring

Begins January 10, 6:30-8:30 PM on campus

Prayer Shawl Group:
100 Shawls Completed (and Counting!)

Begins January 10, 6:30-8:30 PM on campus

What an amazing accomplishment, and labor of love! If you are interested in joining this group, and helping them bless more people through these handmade gifts, please join them every Monday morning from 10 AM-Noon in the Hospitality Center. If you have questions or would like more information about the prayer shawl group, or know of someone ill, grieving, or who may benefit from one of these lovingly handcrafted prayer shawls, please contact Suzi Steele [ email ].

Elementary Kids Church

Sunday Mornings from 10:15-11:45 AM

For all kids K-5th Grade. December's theme in Kids' Church is: Joy - "Finding a way to be happy, even when things don't go your way." Hey, kids – join us this month as we talk about Joy! We'll learn:

  • We can have JOY because God keeps His promises
    (the birth of John the Baptist - Luke 1)
  • We can have JOY because anything is possible with God
    (Gabriel appears to Mary - Luke 1)
  • We can have JOY because God gave us the special gift of His only Son
    (Jesus is born - Luke 2)
  • We can have JOY because God has a plan to make things right
    (Jesus and Simeon - Luke 2)

Each week is made up of small group and large group times with plenty of active involvement to keep things fun and engaging. Hope you can join us
