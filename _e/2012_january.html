A2: Young Adults in Biblical Community

STARTING THIS MONTH...now every Friday
A2 is a Biblical community for young adults (ages 18-30) that meets in homes around the Portland area on Friday nights from 7-8:30 pm. You are invited to join our community as we hang out and have a brief small-group conversation. This is a laid back atmosphere where you can meet friends (old and new) and discuss spiritual topics freely. On the "First Friday" of every month, we gather at the church for an evening of worship and teaching followed by one big meal together. Call Pastor Caleb Bryant's assistant, Wendy Gale, at (503) 245-7735 [ email ] for more information.

Ladies Bible Study 2012

8 Week Study - January 10 - February 28
Tuesdays from 9:30 a.m. - Noon & Wednesdays, 7:00 - 8:30 p.m.
STUDY: "James: Mercy Triumphs" - by Beth Moore
James, Jesus' own brother, started out as a skeptic. See how one glimpse of the resurrected Savior turned an unbeliever into a disciple. Topics in this study include joy, hardship, faith, reversal of fortunes for rich and poor, wisdom, gifts from above, single-mindedness, the dangers of the tongue, humility, and prayer. Water Baptism

Sunday, January 29 during the 10:45 a.m. Sunday Service
Water Baptism is a visible sign to your internal commitment to Jesus. Whether you have been a Christian for years or have just recently committed your life to Christ, we would encourage you to take this next step by being baptized in water. Anyone wanting to be baptized in water can now register online or pick up a registration form at the Information Desk on Sunday mornings. Paper forms should be submitted to the church office by Friday, January 27th. Applicants will be contacted about an informational meeting that will be held prior to the Baptism Service.

Getting Married or Wanting a Marriage Team Check-Up?

Beginning February 4 at 9:00 a.m. we will be offering 8-10 weeks of marriage coaching with the Prepare and Enrich Assessment and MarriageTeam Coaching Program. Facilitated by James & Kathie Frances and Ray & Marybeth Pavlik, these certified coaches will take you through exercises and "team plays" that will strengthen your marriage. Learn how to communicate like never before, work through issues in practical helpful ways, and have the marriage relationship God intended. Discounted fees are for materials only and include a practical workbook and a professional marriage team assessment for $60. For more info, click here or visit MarriageTeams.org. Couples considering marriage, Pre-Marital, Enriched, or Crisis couples are invited to this unique small group experience. Pre-registration recommended.
