JBQ State Finals

"Congrats!" to our three JBQ teams who performed so well at the recent JBQ tournament in Medford, OR. Our A1 team took 1st place, qualifying them for Regionals and Nationals. Top individual honors went to Daniel Duran, Ari Lauthner, Kyle Herring, and Micah Herring. Way to go!
