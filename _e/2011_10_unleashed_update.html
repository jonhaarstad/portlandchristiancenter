This past Sunday, October 9th, the UNLEASHED Bridge Team, consisting of Tom Moore, David Ming, Nathan Bailey and Ron Stokes made an exciting announcement. Listed below is a full report of what was shared.

...let us strip off every weight that slows us down
...and run with endurance the race..."
HEBREWS 12:1

our desire

The desire to eliminate Portland Christian Center’s long-term debt resulted from conversations with the leadership of the church along with invested members and attendees. In leadership meetings, table discussions and town halls we began to envision what we, the church, might look like if we were to function as the full expression of Christ throughout the world.

Our Goal

Significant fluctuations in the local and national economy made our church vulnerable and caused a strain on our departmental budgets and outreach ministries. We soon realized if we paid off the existing debt/mortgage on the church, those funds could be UNLEASHED, without increasing our annual budget, for greater ministry, greater impact, and greater good—all for the glory of God.

Our decision was simple:

Pay the bank through existing loans and subsequent interest or position ourselves to UNLEASH ministry.

And so in April 2011...

...our church family launched into a debt reduction campaign, UNLEASHED, committing through pledges and cash offerings an additional $1,518,000 in giving. This generous act of faith, along with continuing to pay our current mortgage payments, helps Portland Christian Center to be debt free in 3 years and be in a position to UNLEASH the cause of Christ like never before. This is a big task, a grand undertaking, but something we believe God has called us to do.

We formed a committee we call the BRIDGE Team to keep you informed of our progress over the next 3 years on the UNLEASHED campaign. Our BRIDGE Team members consist of Tom Moore (chairman), David Ming, Nathan Bailey, and Ron Stokes. They will give ongoing updates of the UNLEASHED campaign and inspire a stewardship culture within our church.

If you made a pledge towards UNLEASHED, our challenge to you is to continue to be faithful in giving. If you didn’t make a pledge, but would like to get on board with what God is doing, we invite you to join our call to be UNLEASHED for greater ministry.

The Unleashed Bridge Team

TOM MOORE
Committee Lead

For me, UNLEASHED has been an encouragement to step up my game considerably and lean more on God. I find myself quickly and sometimes unconsciously taking on the burden of big and small things that come my way during the day to day journey called life. Sermons earlier this year reminded me of the possibilities if I let God take control of everything. I’ve been experiencing that the more I do this, the more I receive His promise of power, protection, prosperity and presence. Seeing His work in my life and in the Church this year has been an awesome blessing.

DAVID MING

God continues to work in my life in many ways. One way is in the area of stewardship-the responsible planning & management of what God has provided for my family. It doesn’t matter how great or small those blessings are, what matters most is my willingness to be faithful to use them to advance His Kingdom. As we began the Unleashed campaign I had no doubt God wanted me involved. It didn’t matter the amount, it really came down to my willingness to invest my time and money, and exercise the faith I proclaim. I am amazed at the fact that I cannot out-give God. To me, UNLEASHED is not about money, but about what God is able to do when I am faithful to Him.

RON STOKES
Chief Stewardship Officer - Portland Christian Center

To me UNLEASHED has brought about a spiritual experience similar to when I made the commitment to tithe some 37 years ago. The practice of stewardship is in my DNA and is now what I do for God’s Kingdom here at Portland Christian Center. So the natural response for me is to follow God’s direction. And that direction was to make a pledge out of current earnings plus a holy audacious pledge that requires God to supply the resources. That’s the way it was when I began tithing. God supplied then, He will now!

NATHAN BAILEY

The reason my family and I got involved with the UNLEASHED campaign is because, as a young father, it is important to leave a good legacy. I wanted to show my family that stewarding our finances to help fulfill God’s purposes is more important than having the nicest home or the newest car. I wanted to show my family that even though we own a young business, we will trust in God to provide and obey when He tells us to step out in faith. I want the steps Kristi and I take to make a pathway for my kids that allows them to take what we start and build it exponentially so that our story is written out as good and faithful servants.
