We want you to know about an incredible opportunity for your child(ren) this summer. Make the most of the warm weather, free time, and all that energy that the kids have by registering for Young Champions 2010!

Through this exciting track & field camp, your child will build friendship, cultivate awesome sports skills, and build a foundation for life success in a fun environment.

This year, several of our clinic directors are State Finalists in their respective track events and one is the State Champion in the 800m. These enthusiastic athletes will offer focused training, skills development, and instruction in four different track & field events.

Each night, we focus on a different success skill and put it into practice through training for Thursday’s track meet. In addition, the whole family can enjoy the Victory Village BBQ and Carnival as a great way to cap off the fun-filled week.
