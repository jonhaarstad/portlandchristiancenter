July's Featured Events & Activities

Alpha Course

Wednesday Nights from 6:00 - 8:30 PM in the Trailhead

“Is there more to life?” What am I doing here?" “Is this it?” Do you ever find yourself asking questions like these? If so…find out more, with the Alpha Course. Whether you are new to the faith, or been at this for a while, come explore the meaning of life this summer.

Alpha is About...

  • Building friendships with people
  • Interesting conversations around a great meal
  • Finding a place where you can ask perplexing questions
  • Sharing experiences and opinions that lead to greater understanding

Who is Alpha For?
Alpha is open to all people of all ages and backgrounds. If you are open-minded and interested in engaging in conversations regarding your faith then this is a great spot for you. We will look at the basics of the Christian faith and talk about how that relates to our every day lives.

What Can I Expect?
The format is dinner, a short message and discussion. Childcare is provided. There is no charge for the Alpha course - donations for meals and materials are accepted.

How Do I Join?
To sign up for Alpha, you can register online by clicking here and filling out the form. You can also register on Sunday mornings at the Alpha table in the Lobby or at the door during the first four weeks of Alpha. If you want to join after the 4th week of Alpha we recommend waiting until the next course begins. If you have any questions, contact Pastor Joel Slater at JSlater@PCCToday.com.

Active Adults Mt St Helens Day Trip
