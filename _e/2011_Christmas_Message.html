I love this time in December as one year winds down and we get ready for a brand new one full of unlimited Kingdom possibility. As I look back on 2011, I can’t help but think God is well pleased with our Portland Christian Center family. You have been faithful—faithful in loving, in growing, in serving, and in giving! Now, with a new year just around the corner, we continue to need your faithfulness to end this year well so we can start strong in 2012!

Through your generosity in 2011, we have experienced God’s favor in so many ways. It is actually impossible to list them all in this email:

Significant Kingdom Markers

  • 15,000 / 129
    15,000 attended the Christmas Celebration Concerts; 129 crosses were circled indicating salvations - people we can follow up.
  • 100's / 115
    100's have received Christ in our Sunday morning services throughout the year; 115 completed our Alpha discipleship course.
  • We have provided ongoing age-appropriate ministry to our entire church family birth through senior citizens.
  • Our students have been active in local outreach efforts with the Salvation Army, Senior Centers, Teen Challenge, and others.
  • 1,500
    We have touched our city through humanitarian outreach and benevolence, including sponsorship of 1,500 meals to the vulnerable of Portland.
  • 17%
    17% of our church budget has gone towards local and global missions.
  • $100,000+
    Over $100,000 dollars of food and supplies was activated by our partnership with Convoy of Hope to provide for needy families.
  • 65,000
    We advanced the cause of Christ around the world and can account for over 65,000 new believers active in our church plants.
  • We have been faithful in reducing our long-term debt including paying off our corner property, and our shuttle buses.

And that is not all: New people continue to come to our church, seeking hearts continue to be turned toward God, and believers continue to look more like Christ today than they did yesterday.

Thank you for your faithfulness in helping make 2011 a great year. And thank you in advance for enabling this year to finish well so we start the new one with forward momentum. We are looking forward to even greater things in 2012!

Your friend,


Pastor Ray
