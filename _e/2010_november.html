Open Your Heart Giving Campaign

Making this a Season of Giving

We have multiple ways for you to get involved this holiday season in helping others. Visit our Open Your Heart Page for a list of the incredible opportunities we have for you to be a blessing to others.

Holiday Frugalista

November 5, 7:00 – 9:00 PM

Ladies get ready to Dine, Dress and Design on a Dime. Come and learn how to become a Holiday Frugalista at our fall event this November! We'll feature a fashion show with clever ideas to update your holiday wardrobe for $10 and under, a chance to participate in a purse swapping event, and make-up consultants will help complete your look. Sample fabulous food and learn how to present your own "tiny bites". Find frugal ways to revamp furniture and household accessories for the holidays. We’ll show you how to stretch your dollars in ways that will astound you! Bring a friend!

Weekend to Remember Marriage Conference

Discover marriage-changing principles that you can take home and apply to your daily lives that will strengthen your marriage. PCC has special group rates of only $79.50 per person / $159 per couple. Discounted Hotel accommodations are optional. Make plans now to join us for this life-changing event!

PRESCHOOL: Rainbows Club Turkey Time

Wednesday, November 11 – 7:00 – 8:30 PM in the Trailhead

It's Tradition - It's Turkey Time! Calling all Rainbows Club members and their guests to our annual Thanksgiving Celebration. Come hungry and prepare to feast on a kids’ style Thanksgiving meal. Fun games, crafts and a turkey story will also be featured. See you there!

ELEMENTARY: Kids Church

Sunday Mornings from 10:15-11:45 AM

November’s theme is: Uniqueness - "Learning more about others so you can know more about yourself." Hey, kids – join us this month as we talk about uniqueness and memorize I Corinthians 12:4. We'll learn that God made everybody different, God made everybody a somebody, God can use anybody to share His message, and God gave everybody special abilities. Each week is made up of small group and large group times with plenty of active involvement to keep things fun and engaging. Hope you can join us!

MIDDLE SCHOOL:
Father and Son Dodgeball Tournament

Sunday, November 14 from 11:45 AM-3:00 PM

All 6th-8th grade boys and their fathers are invited to join us for an exciting dodgeball tournament! Register in the Youth Center following the main church service, and come prepared to have an awesome time! We’ll provide the lunch! Cost is only $3 per team or $2 each. Be there!

High School & Young Adults Turkey Bowl

Saturday, November 20 from 10:00 AM-2:00 PM

All young adults and high school students are invited to play in our annual Turkey Bowl flag football tournament! We will be meeting at 10 AM at Hamilton Park (come early if you need to pay/register). Cost: $5 per person. Everyone will register individually and there will be a draft to determine your team. You’ll be notified of your team color and captain after the draft. Water, warm drinks and donuts will be provided but we suggest bringing lunch or snacks.

Young Adults Thanksgiving Dinner

Saturday, November 20 from 5:00-9:00 PM

Join us for our annual Thanksgiving feast following the Turkey Bowl! There will be PLENTY of food so come hungry! Cost is $10 for adults, $3 for kids 4 and up, kids 3 and under are FREE! $23 maximum per family. Space is limited so get your tickets ASAP, so you don’t miss out. You must have a ticket in order to eat!

Active Adults Holiday Dinner

Saturday, November 20 at 1:00 PM – On-Campus

Seniors, join us for a lovely evening of food and fellowship! Enjoy a traditional Thanksgiving feast with all the trimmings, while listening to live jazz music! Cost is only $15 per person – tickets must be purchased in advance to attend. Make plans to join us, and bring a friend!
