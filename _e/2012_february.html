A2: Young Adults on Fridays

Every Friday night, young adults from across the city of Portland come together for a time of biblical community. The First Friday of the month, A2 home groups gather together at Portland Christian Center for a combined time of worship, teaching, and a meal. All other Fridays, we meet in local homes around the Portland area to have authentic conversations about faith. Are you searching for something? Do you have questions and cannot find answers? Or are you simply looking for a genuine community to grow in your faith? A2 is a safe place to explore what faith is with other people like you.

First Friday, 7:00 p.m. at Portland Christian Center / Youth Center. If you plan to join us for dinner, the cost is $5 (new visitors eat FREE). Let us know you're coming [RSVP]

Home Groups (all other Fridays), 7:00 p.m. in homes around the Portland area. Contact Wendy Gale [email] for locations or visit us online at pcctoday.com/A2. Have a question about what we're about? Contact Pastor Caleb Bryant (Young Adults Pastor). He would love to have coffee with you and hear your story. [email].
